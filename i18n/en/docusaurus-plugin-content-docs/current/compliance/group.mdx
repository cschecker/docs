# Group Management

To manage groups, click `Inspection>Inspection Management`.
To perform an inspection, you must `create a Group`.

## Creation

1. Click the `Add Icon` to create a Group.

    <img width="200" src={group1} alt="img" />

2. Enter the Group information. <br/>
    <img width="700" src={group2} alt="img" />
    <br />
    2-1. Enter the Group name. (within 20 characters)
    <br />
    2-2. Enter the Group description. (within 100 characters)
    <br />
    2-3. Select the CSP. (only one CSP can be set per Group.)
    <br />
    2-4. Select the compliance.
    <br />
    2-5. Select the assets to add to the Group.
    <br />
    2-6. Click the Add Group button.

<p>&nbsp;</p>

## Edit

1. Click the `Three-dot Icon` of the Group whose name you want to edit, then click `Rename Group`.

    <img width="300" src={group3} alt="img" />

2. Enter the Group name and click the `Change Button`.

    <img width="500" src={group4} alt="img" />

<p>&nbsp;</p>

## Delete

1. Click the `Three-dot Icon` of the Group you want to delete, then click `Delete Group`.

    <img width="300" src={group3} alt="img" />

2. Enter the Group name and click the `Delete Button`.

    <img width="600" src={group5} alt="img" />
