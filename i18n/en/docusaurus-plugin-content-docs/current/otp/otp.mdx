## Registration

1. Install the google `authenticator` app via the provided link.
2. Open the google authenticator app and scan the `QR code`.
3. Once issued, the authentication key cannot be retrieved again, so be sure to write it down.
4. Enter the `6-digit verification code` registered in the google authenticator app and click the `Complete Verification Button`.

## Modification

1. Click `Edit OTP` to modify your OTP.

    <img width="200" src={otp6} alt="img" />

2. Enter the `OTP verification code`.

    <img width="400" src={otp1} alt="img" />

3. Install the `Google Authenticator app` on your phone, then click the `Next Button`.

    <img width="400" src={otp2} alt="img" />

4. Open Google Authenticator and register your account. The `Next Button` will be activated only after you check the checkbox confirming the account registration is complete.

    <img width="400" src={otp3} alt="img" />

5. Once lost, the authentication key cannot be retrieved. It is recommended to print or write it down.

    <img width="400" src={otp4} alt="img" />

6. Enter the newly registered authentication code and click `Complete Verification`.

    <img width="400" src={otp5} alt="img" />
