# Login

<img width="400" src={login1} alt="img" />

1. Enter the `Email`.
2. Enter the `Password`.

<img width="400" src={login2} alt="img" />

3. When the OTP authentication popup appears, open the Google Authenticator app on your phone to check the code.
4. Enter the code you checked.(If you lost the authentication key, please contact KISMI. Email: help@kismi.kr)
5. Click the `Login Button`.
