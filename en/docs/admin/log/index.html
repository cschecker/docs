Log

Logs are provided for six categories: Assets, Inspections, Users, Sub-Organization, Organization Management, and Credentials. You can specify a desired time period to view the logs.

To view detailed logs, click the Magnifying Glass Icon.

Additional information can be checked in the log details popup.
